TinyBot

A locally running AI chatbot powered by Ollama + TinyLlama

How to use: Type any question into the box below and click Send. TinyBot will send your message to a local Flask server, which passes it to Ollama running on your machine. The response may take a few seconds the first time. 

    


    

      
Suggested Prompts

      

        What should I eat for lunch?
        Explain how the internet works
        Write me a haiku about coding
        What is the request-response cycle?
        Give me a fun fact
